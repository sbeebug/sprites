Gem Hunter

Goal: Find the hidden gemstones

In my game, you play as an explorer who discovers a secret cave. You must navigate through different passageways within the cave. Equipted with a coin and a lighter, the player makes choices on where to go and looks to find clues. Each clue will lead to the next clue and weave the player through tunnels. Leave it up to chance and flip a coin, or use your lighter to look closely at different areas. The player wins when they find the hidden gemstone at the end of the tunnels.
